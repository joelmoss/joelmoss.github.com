Heat Mapping

Mar 9th, 2006

Found a very promising site today; CrazyEgg seems to be an analytics service for sites, but with a twist. It will show you a screenshot of your site, but with heat patterns overlaid. These heat patterns let you know what parts of your site are cliucked on the most. The hotter the area, the more it is clicked. Very nice idea!

I've already registered my interest and await more info.

Firefox Made $72M Last Year?!

Mar 7th, 2006

Absolutely unbelievable, but apparently, Mozilla, the makers of fast growing Firefox browser made $72 million last year. This was mainly collected from Google and other affiliates. If you search using the default search bix on top right of Firefox, you end up at Google and if you click on any of the sponsored ads, Mozilla get 80% of the ads fees.

So, anyone fancy making a browser and a couple of million?
