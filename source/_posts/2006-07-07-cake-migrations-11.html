--- layout: post title: Cake Migrations 1.1 categories: articles ---

I have released a new version of Cake Migrations. With thanks to Evan Broder, Migrations now support additional column types: ENUM and BOOL. It also supports the creation and deltion of Keys and altering columns.

You can get version 1.1 of Cake Migrations from CakeForge. More info on Cake Migrations and what they are can be found in the Cake Wiki.

Enjoy ;)
