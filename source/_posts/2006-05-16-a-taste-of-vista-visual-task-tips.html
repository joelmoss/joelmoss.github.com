A little taster of things to came in the new Windows Vista and a touch of what MAC users have enjoyed for a while.

Visual Task Tips display a small thumbnail preview of each program running in your task bar. Simply roll over any program in your task bar and you get a great looking screen shot of that application.

Completely useless, but pretty cool ;)
