The very first release of Switchboard can now be seen in use at http://switchboard.homepageuniverse.com

The HPU:Community will be the exclusive user of Switchboard until the public beta begins later this month (August), and will enable us to run some real world testing and perhaps gauge a little interest and feedback.

We will also be launching the new Tooum.com site in a few days, which will include all the details about Tooum and Switchboard, aswell as a full public demo of Switchboard.

So take a sneak peek now – http://switchboard.homepageuniverse.com
