--- layout: post title: Cake no longer uses its own shortcuts categories: articles ---

Well it appears that Cake no longer uses its own function shortcuts within the core. As of changeset 6128 all usage of the good old low/up, am, r and friends, are no longer used within the core. However, these functions still exist for you to use in your own code.

So the question I ask is; why are these functions no longer good enough for the core? Are they soon to be deprecated? (I hope not).

It's an interesting move nevertheless.
